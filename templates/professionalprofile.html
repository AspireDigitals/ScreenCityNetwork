Who We Are?

SCN network is a market place for movie scripts where you can buy, sell, or hire a professional screenplay writer It takes less than 2 minutes to post a gig on SCN.

You can sign up for a free account and post ads easily every time. Script city Network does not specialize in any specific category - here you can buy and sell items in all aspect of film and writing/script categories. We also carefully review all ads that are being published, to make sure the quality is up to our standards.
